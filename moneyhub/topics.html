Topics & Guides

The areas I get asked about most

These are the areas I get asked about most. I'll keep adding more over time.

Budgeting & Cash Flow

How to see where your money is actually going without turning your life into a spreadsheet nightmare. I like simple categories and clear rules instead of tracking every coffee.

  • Budgeting basics and how I structure categories.
  • Handling irregular expenses (car repairs, travel, yearly bills).
  • Deciding how much you can safely save and invest each month.

Saving & Safety Nets

Cash isn't exciting, but it keeps you from getting knocked out of the game. I treat safety nets as a separate question from investing: how much do I need to sleep at night?

  • How I decide emergency fund size.
  • Where I keep short-term vs long-term cash.
  • Balancing security with not sitting on too much idle money.

Investing

I'm a long-term investor. I think in years and decades, not trading days. I care about asset allocation, tax efficiency, and having conviction in what I own so I can hold through volatility.

  • How I think about 401(k), Roth IRA, and taxable accounts together.
  • Why I focus on simple, high-conviction ideas rather than constant trading.
  • How I handle market drawdowns without panicking.

Advanced / Deep Dives

  • Thinking about generational wealth and long-term compounding.
